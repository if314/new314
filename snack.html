Snack4 snack

Snack 4 snack is a concept native application I created as a personal project while being laid off due to COVIT-19, designed to keep you up to date with the snacking options at your office.

Problem

Users are upset when they don't feel like eating the office snacks they brought. Going around the office from desk to desk asking colleagues, what snacks they got today can be time-consuming.

Goal

Feed the hungry coworkers.
Solution
