Optimizing workload distribution

Context

Aspirion is a healthcare revenue cycle company that helps healthcare providers recover complex and denied claims. Associates rely on Aspiron's Compass platform to do this work, but the training and onboarding system created confusion and slowed proficiency. I led a redesign that turned training into an empowering experience through stakeholder co-creation and systems

Process

  1. Visualize the current traninig system Mapped the existing training system to uncover root causes.

  2. Humanaize the System Engaged stakeholders through interviews and co-design sessions to identify leverage points and align goals.

  3. Redesign the system Prototyped and tested redesigned solutions

Visualize the System

We visualize the system by creating network, process, and problem maps. Allowing us to understand complex connections and stakeholder relationships, analyze processes from various perspectives, and address root causes rather than symptoms..

Humanize the System

User interviews revealed deep mistrust in the training system. New hires viewed it as outdated, unreliable, and inconsistent. Most relied on personal networks over official resources. By mapping these behaviors, we reframed the challenge.

The Redesign

Prioritization matrix

We met with stakeholders to review the maps and share insights. Through co-design discussions, we identified opportunities to improve the system by updating structure, processes, infrastructure, talent, and incentives.

Next steps

Given users’ familiarity with in-app onboarding from other platforms, we proposed enhancing our application with similar techniques. This concept was categorized as medium effort with high potential impact.
The team then evaluated two paths forward: integrating a third-party onboarding tool or developing a custom in-house solution.

Identify a Potential Prototype

We tested several onboarding tools, including Chameleon and Pendo. Chameleon supported our early needs, but after the acquisition and a larger budget, we moved to Pendo for its stronger analytics.

Prototype Testing

Objectives

When prototyping, the goal is not only to validate the idea but also to confirm how it fits within the system and to plan the next steps strategically.

Reflection

The findings indicate that implementing in-app onboarding and guides improves outcomes and enhances user confidence in their job. Additionally, discrepancies in vocabulary across teams highlight the importance of standardizing terminology for clearer communication.
