How I Work
systems-first, execution-focused

01

Model the system

Make complexity explicit by mapping inputs, states, dependencies, and edge cases before committing to UI.

  • State and decision models
  • Dependency and risk mapping
  • Edge cases and recovery paths
02

Prototype the behavior

Define hierarchy, transitions, and feedback loops with interactive prototypes so intent is unambiguous.

  • Interaction states and transitions
  • Hierarchy and clarity
  • Usability validation and iteration
03

Ship with quality

Partner with engineering to protect fidelity through build, QA, and iteration, with performance in mind.

  • Build-ready specs and standards
  • Design QA in development
  • Metrics and post-launch tuning
